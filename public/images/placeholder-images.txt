2. wildlife-placeholder.jpg - A photo of wildlife like an elk or deer
3. birds-blooms-placeholder.jpg - A photo of a bird with flowers
4. butterfly-placeholder.jpg - A photo of a butterfly on a flower
5. weathered-wood-placeholder.jpg - A close-up of weathered wood texture
6. stained-glass-placeholder.jpg - A photo of colorful stained glass

- Unsplash (https://unsplash.com)
